Societies

Why Join a Society?

Societies are a great way to make friends and attend weekly social events. There are over 200 societies to choose from including Cheese & Wine Society, 20-Minute Society and Irish Dance. Joining a course based societies will allow you to meet others from your chosen subject.

Volunteering

Why Volunteer?

"Want to help out your local community? Go Volunteer will allow you to develop employability skills and make new friends. Here is a flavour of the types of activities you can get involved with...
N.E.S.T helps local refugee and asylum-seekers through English classes, football, arts and craft and days out.
Helping Our Homeless Society offers educational workshops and distributes supplies to homeless people in Newcastle.
Beekeeping will allow you to experience beekeeping first hand right here on campus!
For more information on the opportunities available please visit the Go Volunteer Website

Sports Clubs

Why Join a Sports Club?

NUSU has over 65 sports clubs to get involved with, including American Football and Water Polo! These clubs welcome everyone, regardless of your fitness levels or experience. There is even opportunity to participate in Campus League competitions against other universities.

Student Media

What is Student Media?

Love Journalism, Media or Radio? Then you should definitely get involved in one of the University's Student Media teams.
Newcastle Student Radio is online 24/7 with shows produced by students. You can learn about all aspects of radio, including presenting, producing or behind the scenes opportunities. Contact them here: http://thisisnsr.co.uk/contacts/
The Courrier Newspaper offers students the opportunity for their voices to be heard, regardless of prior journalistic experience.
NUTV produce television content on a huge variety of content, such as current affairs, lifestyle and entertainment. There are plenty of opportunities to get involved and make industry connections. Our award-winning Student Media team is always hot on the heels for the latest coverage.

Want to try something new? Then Give It a Go! Try different activities every month such as sports, socials and days out and make new friends.
